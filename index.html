Privacy Policy for Protein Clicker

Last updated: June 24, 2026

"Protein Clicker" (the "App") is developed and published by PiGenGamingStudios ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use the App and how it is used.

Contact: PiGenGamingStudios@gmail.com

1. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App lets you log your daily protein intake across several categories and set a daily goal. All of this data (dates, amounts, goals) is stored only in a local database on your device. It is never transmitted to us or to any third party, and no account or sign-in is required. If you uninstall the App or use the in-app reset function, this data is permanently deleted. The App also uses this local data to decide whether to show you the daily reminder notification described in Section 5 (for example, it will not remind you if you have already logged protein that day) — this check happens entirely on your device.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob. To do this, Google's Mobile Ads SDK may collect and process information such as:

  • Advertising ID (a resettable device identifier)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Device information (model, operating system, language)
  • App usage and interaction data related to ads

This data is processed by Google, which acts as an independent data controller for this processing. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy and how Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services.

3. Consent for Personalized Ads (EEA / UK / Switzerland)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, the App uses Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to ask for your consent before showing personalized ads, in line with GDPR requirements. You can review or change your choice at any time via the "Privacy Options" link shown above the ad banner in the App.

4. In-App Purchases (Google Play Billing)

The App offers an optional one-time "Remove Ads" purchase, processed entirely through Google Play Billing. We do not receive or store your payment details. The App stores only a local flag on your device indicating whether you purchased this option, so it can hide ads on future launches and after reinstalling (automatically restored via Google Play).

5. Permissions

The App requests the following Android permissions:

  • INTERNET – required to load ads and communicate with Google Play Billing
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE – allows the ad SDK to check network connectivity before requesting ads
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS – allows the App to show an optional daily reminder to log your protein intake. This notification is scheduled and shown entirely on your device; no data about it is collected or sent to us or any third party. You can deny or revoke this permission at any time in your device's notification settings without affecting any other App functionality.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. The only third party involved in the App is Google, for the purposes of advertising and in-app billing described above.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your Rights

Since the App does not require an account and stores your tracking data only locally, you can delete all of it simply by uninstalling the App or using the in-app reset function. For data processed by Google (advertising ID, ad personalization consent), you can manage your choices via the "Privacy Options" link in the App or through your device's Google "Ads Settings".

If you are located in the EEA or UK, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, and to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at PiGenGamingStudios@gmail.com.
